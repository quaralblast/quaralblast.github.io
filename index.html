View Synthesis Across Cultures: Evaluating Model Performance in Diverse Visual Contexts

This experiment tested the generalizability of novel View Synthesis models when it came to diverse cultural contexts. We hypothesized that models would perform worse on scenes from non-eurocentric places. Instead, we found that scene complexity was likely a bigger hindrance to model performance.
